    About Us

    WideSchool is the leading platform for researching schools in
    Rwanda. With thousands of users each month, wideschool.com is
    the platform where students and families find information on schools.

    Our Ambition at wideschool.com is to have every school in Rwanda
    listed on the platform. This is an ambitious goal, and we are building
    partnerships with key players in the education sector in order to achieve it.
    We are continuously striving to grow the number of schools listed, as well
    as improve the quality of the information we provide.
